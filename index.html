Privacy Policy for LifeClock - Age Counter

Last updated: 02-12-2024

Information Collection and Use

LifeClock - Age Counter collects the following information:

  • Birth date: This information is required to calculate your age in real-time.

Data Storage

All data collected by LifeClock is:

  • Stored locally on your device using Chrome's storage API
  • Never transmitted to external servers
  • Never shared with third parties
  • Only used for the purpose of age calculation

User Control

Users have full control over their data:

  • You can view your stored birth date at any time through the extension interface
  • You can modify your birth date at any time
  • You can delete your data by clearing the extension storage or uninstalling the extension

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
